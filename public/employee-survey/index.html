About the Framework

What Is the Gallup Q12 Employee Engagement Survey?

The Gallup Q12 is a set of 12 scientifically validated questions developed by Gallup through decades of workplace research spanning millions of employees across industries and countries. It is widely regarded as the gold standard for measuring employee engagement — the degree to which employees feel involved, enthusiastic, and committed to their work.

Engaged employees are more productive, less likely to leave, and more likely to act as advocates for their organization. Research consistently shows that companies in the top quartile of employee engagement outperform their peers on key business metrics including profitability, customer satisfaction, and absenteeism.

By using this free tool, your organization can benchmark engagement levels, identify gaps, and take targeted action — the same approach used by Fortune 500 companies and leading Indian enterprises.
