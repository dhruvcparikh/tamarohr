  • Challan Generation: Prepare EPF and ESI challans with exact amounts due
  • New Member Registration: UAN generation, ESI registration for new employees
  • Exit Formalities: PF withdrawal, transfer, final settlement processing
  • Inspection Support: Documentation and support during EPF/ESI inspections

Professional Tax & TDS

  • PT Deduction: State-wise PT calculation and monthly deduction
  • PT Return Filing: Monthly/quarterly PT returns as per state regulations
  • TDS Calculation: Accurate TDS computation based on regime selection
  • TDS Filing: Monthly TDS payment, quarterly returns (24Q) filing
  • Form 16 Generation: Annual Form 16 (Part A & B) for all employees

Bonus & Gratuity

  • Bonus Calculation: Annual bonus computation as per Payment of Bonus Act
  • Bonus Register: Maintain statutory bonus registers and records
  • Gratuity Calculation: Accurate gratuity computation for exits and retirements
  • Gratuity Payment: Process payments within statutory timelines
  • Form R Return: Annual gratuity return filing with authorities

Other Compliance

  • Shops & Establishments: Registration, renewals, and compliance management
  • Labour Welfare Fund: LWF deduction and payment where applicable
  • Minimum Wages: Ensure compliance with state minimum wages
  • Maternity Benefit: Calculation and payment as per Maternity Benefit Act
  • Registers & Records: Maintain all statutory registers as per law
